# Introduction

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics book. This comprehensive guide covers the essential concepts, tools, and techniques needed to develop intelligent robotic systems with a focus on humanoid robots.

This book is organized into five modules that progressively build your understanding of robotics, from fundamental concepts to advanced AI integration and deployment.

## About This Book

This book provides:

- **Theoretical foundations** of robotics and AI
- **Practical implementations** with real code examples
- **Integration techniques** for complex robotic systems
- **Best practices** for developing reliable robotic applications

## Target Audience

This book is designed for:
- Robotics engineers and researchers
- AI/ML practitioners working on embodied systems
- Students studying robotics and AI
- Developers building robotic applications

## Prerequisites

To get the most out of this book, you should have:
- Basic programming experience (Python preferred)
- Understanding of mathematics (linear algebra, calculus)
- Familiarity with robotics concepts (optional but helpful)
